EBA Guidelines on ESG Risk Management

REGULATORY FRAMEWORK
The European Banking Authority (EBA) provides guidelines for:
- ESG risk management and supervision
- Climate risk stress testing
- ESG disclosure requirements
- Sustainable finance integration

ESG RISK MANAGEMENT PRINCIPLES
1. Governance and Strategy
   - Board oversight of ESG risks
   - ESG risk appetite definition
   - Integration into business strategy
   - Stakeholder engagement

2. Risk Identification and Assessment
   - ESG risk taxonomy development
   - Materiality assessment
   - Risk measurement methodologies
   - Data quality and availability

3. Risk Management and Controls
   - ESG risk limits and thresholds
   - Risk mitigation strategies
   - Monitoring and reporting frameworks
   - Internal control systems

4. Disclosure and Transparency
   - ESG risk disclosure requirements
   - Reporting standards alignment
   - Stakeholder communication
   - Regulatory reporting

CLIMATE RISK STRESS TESTING
Requirements for climate stress testing:
- Multiple scenario analysis (2°C, 4°C, disorderly transition)
- Time horizons: short-term (1-3 years), medium-term (3-10 years)
- Risk transmission channels analysis
- Impact assessment on capital adequacy

SUSTAINABLE FINANCE INTEGRATION
Guidelines for sustainable finance:
- Green asset ratio calculation
- EU Taxonomy alignment assessment
- Sustainable finance product development
- ESG investment strategy implementation

ESG RISK CATEGORIES
Environmental Risks:
- Climate change risks (physical and transition)
- Natural resource depletion
- Biodiversity loss
- Pollution and waste management

Social Risks:
- Human rights violations
- Labor standards and working conditions
- Community relations
- Data privacy and security

Governance Risks:
- Corporate governance failures
- Corruption and bribery
- Regulatory compliance
- Business ethics

RISK MEASUREMENT METHODOLOGIES
Quantitative approaches:
- ESG scoring models
- Risk-adjusted return calculations
- Stress testing scenarios
- Portfolio impact analysis

Qualitative approaches:
- ESG risk assessments
- Materiality analysis
- Stakeholder engagement
- Expert judgment

DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS
ESG risk disclosure should include:
- Governance arrangements for ESG risks
- Strategy for managing ESG risks
- Risk management processes
- Metrics and targets for ESG risks

SUPERVISORY EXPECTATIONS
Supervisors should:
- Assess ESG risk management frameworks
- Review ESG risk identification processes
- Evaluate ESG risk measurement methodologies
- Monitor ESG risk disclosure quality

IMPLEMENTATION GUIDANCE
Phase 1: ESG risk identification and assessment
Phase 2: Integration into risk management frameworks
Phase 3: Development of measurement methodologies
Phase 4: Implementation of disclosure requirements

CAPACITY BUILDING
EBA supports capacity building through:
- Technical guidance and best practices
- Training and education programs
- Industry collaboration and knowledge sharing
- International cooperation on ESG risk management

MONITORING AND REPORTING
Regular monitoring of:
- ESG risk management implementation
- Progress on ESG risk integration
- Quality of ESG risk disclosures
- Effectiveness of ESG risk controls
