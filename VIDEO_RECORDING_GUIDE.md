## **Recording Tips**

### **Technical Setup:**
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Audio**: Clear microphone, no background noise
- **Browser**: Chrome or Edge for best rendering

### **Presentation Style:**
- **Pace**: Speak clearly, not too fast
- **Tone**: Professional and confident
- **Enthusiasm**: Show genuine excitement for the solution
- **Clarity**: Explain technical concepts simply

### **Visual Flow:**
- **Smooth transitions** between sections
- **Consistent cursor movement**
- **Highlight important elements** with cursor
- **Avoid rapid scrolling** or clicking
