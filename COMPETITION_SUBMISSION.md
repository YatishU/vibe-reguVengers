## **2. Project Details & Innovation**

### **Use Case for Coding:**
**Revolutionizing ESG Compliance Through Intelligent Automation**

Dutch banks face unprecedented regulatory pressure from CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive), EU Taxonomy, and TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures). Manual ESG analysis is time-consuming, error-prone, and doesn't scale. ESG Copilot addresses this critical gap by providing AI-powered document analysis, real-time compliance monitoring, and predictive risk detection.

The platform leverages cutting-edge **Large Language Models (LLM)** and **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** to automate the analysis of complex ESG documents including CSRD Double Materiality Assessments, EU Taxonomy Alignment Disclosures, and Climate Risk Stress Test Reports. It identifies compliance gaps, detects risk drift, and provides actionable recommendations to ensure banks meet regulatory requirements while driving sustainable growth.

### **AI/ML & RAG Capabilities:**

**🧠 Advanced Language Model Integration:**
- **Azure OpenAI GPT-4**: Intelligent document parsing and ESG content extraction
- **Natural Language Understanding**: Semantic analysis of regulatory requirements and compliance gaps
- **Content Generation**: Automated report generation and recommendation creation
- **Multi-language Support**: Analysis of ESG documents in multiple European languages

**🔍 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):**
- **Vector Database**: Pinecone/Weaviate integration for semantic search across ESG regulatory frameworks
- **Knowledge Base**: Pre-populated with CSRD, EU Taxonomy, TCFD, and UN SDG documentation
- **Context-Aware Responses**: Intelligent answers based on current regulatory context and historical compliance data
- **Real-time Updates**: Dynamic knowledge base updates as regulations evolve

**📊 Intelligent Document Processing:**
- **Document Embedding**: Vector representations of ESG documents for semantic similarity analysis
- **Regulatory Mapping**: Automatic alignment of bank disclosures with EU sustainability frameworks
- **Compliance Scoring**: AI-driven assessment using weighted ESG criteria and regulatory requirements
